Quick Puff Pastry Recipe

A quick version of the classic flakey dough. Prep time is 20 minutes plus chilling time.

Ingredients:

  • 360g Unbleached All-purpose flour
  • 340g Unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 227g cold water

Instructions

  1. Using the flat beater attachment, mix the flour and cold butter on low speed until the mixture is unevenly crumbly.
  2. In a separate bowl, dissolve the salt in the water and add to the flour mixture.
  3. Mix on low speep until the dough becomes a shaggy mass, crumbly with small chunks of butter.
  4. Turn the dough on a lightly floured surface and knead it until it comes together.
  5. Shape the dough into an 8" square, wrap in plastic wrap, and refridgerate for 30 minutes
  6. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into an 8" x 20" rectangle.
  7. Letter fold the dough (fold into thirds), turn it 90° and roll into another 8" x 20" rectangle.
  8. Book fold the dough and turn it two times. If the dough becomes difficult to roll or fold, chill it for 5-10 minutes.
  9. After the last book fold, chill the dough for 30 minutes before using.

The dough can be stored in the refridgerator for a day or in the freezer for a month.
